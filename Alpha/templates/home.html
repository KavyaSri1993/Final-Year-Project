Step 1: Recognizing Unusual URLs

Learn how to identify suspicious URLs that may lead to phishing sites.

Tips:

  • Look for misspellings or unusual domain names.
  • Check for additional words or numbers that seem out of place.
  • Always hover over links to see the actual URL before clicking.
